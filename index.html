//Base format

Base format

The course is suitable for both beginners and masters who have little theoretical knowledge and experience

Duration: 1 week

Number of offline lessons: 3

Cost of this format: 55000 rub

Base course program

1 day (~3-4 hours)

  • Theory
  • General study of equipment, its assembly and setup. Sanitary requirements, contraindications, disinfection
  • General advice on materials
  • Preparation of the workplace and space, safety standards
  • Assistance with the selection of consumables and power equipment
  • Individual consultation, review of the best brands
  • Working with painkillers (specifics, nuances)
  • Healing methods

2 day (~2-3 hours)

  • Theory
  • Tattoo styles. (direction selection)
  • Development of sketches, composition
  • The specifics of the anatomical location on the body, features, distortions
  • Methods for applying and transferring a sketch to the skin
  • Features of the structure of the skin. Difference of skin, on different parts of the body
  • Healing, pigment migration, its distortion
  • Practice on artificial skin
  • Technical aspects of tattooing
  • Setting the hand, working on the mistakes

3 day (~3 hours)

  • Checking theoretical knowledge
  • Self-preparation of the place
  • Selection and development of the sketch on the model
  • Contraindications and tattoo care
